A Short Group Video Exploring Personal Emotions During COVID-19

A reflective storytelling piece centered on personal experience and emotional truth.

Structure

Group work (3 team members)

Role

Team member

Tool

Smartphone recording, WeVideo

Timeline

2 weeks

Responsibility

Content creation, video recording, editing

Overview

This project is a short group video created as a storytelling exercise focused on personal emotions experienced during the COVID-19 pandemic. The video explores how isolation, uncertainty, and changes in daily life affected us on an emotional level. The project emphasizes authenticity and personal expression rather than polished production, allowing the story and emotions to remain central.

Content & Storytelling

The video was developed collaboratively by a three-person team. We worked together on shaping the narrative, sharing personal reflections, and deciding how to visually communicate our experiences.

Footage was recorded using smartphones to capture a more natural and relatable tone. The final edit was completed using WeVideo, focusing on pacing and coherence rather than heavy visual effects.
