Flower Online Store Website

Designing a Simple and Functional E-commerce Website for a Local Flower Shop

A clean storefront experience that highlights floral products and keeps shopping intuitive.

Structure

Individual work

Tool

Wix

Timeline

4 weeks

Responsibility

UX design, Content structure, client communication

Overview

This project involved designing and building an online store website for a flower shop to support product browsing and online purchasing. The focus was on creating a clean, visually appealing experience that highlights floral products while keeping the shopping flow simple and intuitive.

The website was built using Wix, allowing for fast iteration and close collaboration with the client throughout the process.

Client Communication & Project Goals

The project began with direct communication with the client to understand their business needs, brand preferences, and expectations for the website. The client wanted an online presence that felt elegant and seasonal, while remaining easy to update and manage without technical support.

Through these early conversations, we aligned on a few key goals:

Showcase flower arrangements with strong visual focus. Highlight seasonal collections and best-selling products. Keep navigation simple for quick browsing and purchasing. Use a platform that allows the client to maintain content independently.

These discussions helped define both the scope of the project and the level of complexity needed for the website.

Design Approach

Based on the client‚Äôs needs, the design prioritizes clarity and visual balance. Large product images, minimal interface elements, and generous spacing were used to let the flowers remain the focal point of each page.

The homepage highlights featured collections and best sellers, while the product listing page organizes items by category with straightforward filtering to support easy discovery.
