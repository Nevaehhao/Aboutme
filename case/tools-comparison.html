Adobe Photoshop, Pixelmator Pro & Affinity Photo Comparison Video

Creating an Educational Comparison Video for Image Editing Tools

A practical comparison that highlights real-world workflows, pricing, and outcomes.

Structure

Group work (3 team members)

Tool

Adobe Photoshop, Pixelmator Pro, Affinity Photo, DaVinci Resolve

Timeline

3 weeks

Responsibility

Comparison content writing, video editing, voice recording

Overview

This project is a group-created comparison video that introduces and contrasts Adobe Photoshop, Pixelmator Pro, and Affinity Photo. The video focuses on practical use cases such as meme creation and pricing differences, helping viewers understand how each tool performs in real-world scenarios.

Content Focus

The comparison emphasizes real-world scenarios rather than theoretical feature lists. Each tool was explored through hands-on examples to show how similar tasks are handled differently, making the comparison accessible to both beginners and casual users. Pricing models and value differences were also included to support more informed decision-making.

Production & Editing

The video was edited in DaVinci Resolve, with attention to pacing, clarity, and visual consistency. A professional microphone was used for voice recording to ensure clear audio quality and improve the overall viewing experience.
