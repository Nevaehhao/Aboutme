Sequence Film Editing

Collaborative Video Editing with Music and Visual Rhythm

A short sequence film crafted through collaborative editing and musical timing.

Structure

Group work (3 team members)

Role

Team member

Tool

WeVideo

Timeline

1 week

Responsibility

Video editing, sequencing, music synchronization

Overview

This project is a short sequence film created through collaborative video editing. The goal was to combine multiple video clips into a coherent sequence and enhance the overall rhythm and mood through music.

All editing work was completed collaboratively by the team, with shared responsibility for clip selection, sequencing, and timing.

Editing Focus

The editing emphasizes continuity and pacing, using music to guide transitions and structure the flow of the sequence. Attention was given to visual rhythm, cut timing, and alignment between motion and sound to create a smooth and engaging viewing experience. WeVideo was used as the primary editing tool, allowing the team to collaborate efficiently and iterate on edits together.
